LANGCHAIN MASTER HANDBOOK

VOLUME 1 : RETRIEVAL AUGMENTED GENERATION (RAG)

================================================= 1. WHAT IS RAG?
=================================================

RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) is an AI architecture that
combines:

1.  Information Retrieval
2.  Large Language Model Generation

Instead of relying only on the model’s internal knowledge, RAG retrieves
relevant documents from an external knowledge base and uses them as
context before generating an answer.

Why RAG? - Solves hallucination - Uses private/company documents -
Supports frequently updated knowledge - Avoids expensive retraining -
Handles very large document collections

================================================= 2. WHY RAG IS NEEDED
=================================================

Problems with LLMs - Knowledge cutoff - Hallucinations - No access to
private data - Limited context window

Solutions - Fine-tuning - RAG

Fine-tuning changes model weights. RAG keeps model weights unchanged and
supplies external context.

================================================= 3. COMPLETE RAG
PIPELINE =================================================

Documents ↓ Document Loader ↓ Text Splitter ↓ Embeddings ↓ Vector Store
↓ Retriever ↓ Relevant Chunks ↓ Prompt Augmentation ↓ LLM ↓ Final Answer

The Cricket Song

On a field so wide and green,
The finest game is always seen.
A bat held firm, a shining ball,
The crowd awaits each thrilling call.

The bowler runs with steady pace,
A hopeful smile upon the face.
The batter stands with fearless might,
Prepared to play with all their might.

A cover drive, so smooth and bright,
The ball takes off in pure delight.
Across the field it swiftly flies,
Beneath the clear and sunny skies.

The fielders chase with endless pride,
Together standing side by side.
A diving catch, a joyful cheer,
The sound of victory fills the air.

Sixes soar beyond the ground,
Loud applause becomes the sound.
Boundaries bring a smile so wide,
Fans celebrate with endless pride.

Cricket teaches heart and grace,
Teamwork wins the toughest race.
With every match and every day,
It shows the true and sporting way.

So let the bat and ball unite,
From morning sun till fading light.
For cricket is a timeless art,
A game that lives in every heart.
